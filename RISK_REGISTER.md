# Risk Register — Atlas

**Status:** Canonical engineering risk register (architecture-phase closure set). Covers engineering, operational, performance, security, maintainability risks, and unknowns — each with probability, impact, and mitigation.
**Doctrine note on probabilities:** project doctrine forbids confidence without evidence. Probabilities below are **planning judgments** (High/Medium/Low) with their basis stated; where doctrine forbids a forecast outright (spike outcome), probability is recorded as **None-assessable** and the register plans for all branches instead.
**Relationship to frozen risks:** R1–R6 (`ASSUMPTIONS_AND_RISKS.md`) are product risks, frozen and not re-scored here. This register records how the *architecture and plan* absorb them, plus the engineering-level risks the frozen package does not enumerate.
