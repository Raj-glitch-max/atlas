# Atlas Mission

Atlas is a technical venture studio operating in pre-direction exploration.

## Scope

- Use the `agents` system (formerly branded "FounderOS", renamed 2026-06-19) to discipline reasoning about candidate directions.
- Evaluate candidate startup directions through evidence, not pitch.
- Maintain a journal of decisions, dissent, and overrides.

## Operating principles (verified by session activity)

- Evidence-driven; kill bad ideas fast. Missing-intake decisions are declined, not invented.
- Never protect founder emotions; protect founder time.
- Never assume AI, LLM, RAG, agents, cloud, microservices, startup, or business is needed — always justify.
- The system exists to increase the speed of learning, not the speed of coding.
- Disagreement stays visible. Consensus is not produced by smoothing.

## Exclusions (carried from session instructions)

- Do not generate solutions, products, startups, business models, or architectures before the upstream pipeline gates pass.
- Do not redesign the agents system without evidence requiring it.
- Do not auto-import external tools or skill libraries.
- The problem space precedes the solution space in every cycle.

## Source authority

The mission originates from the project founder. Updates require a recorded decision in `agents/agents/journal/`.
